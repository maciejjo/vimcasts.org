VimCasts.org

Vimcasts.org now has it's own short form URLs. You can link to each episode by number, using the form /e/10 to link to episode 10. You can also link to the episodes archive with the trailing path /e/a.

These short form URLs are perfect for sharing on Twitter, and in other situations where characters count. At the end of each screencast, I can now include a short form URL so that you can easily find the corresponding show notes for an episode.
